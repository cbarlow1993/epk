ls ./features/

      
Everything you need to build a professional DJ press kit, configured in one place.

    

    

      

        

          

          bio.config
        

        

          
Artist Bio

          
Rich text biography editor with markdown support. Tell your story the way you want it read.

        

      

      

        

          

          music.config
        

        

          
Music Embeds

          
SoundCloud, Spotify, Mixcloud, Bandcamp. Embed your tracks and mixes with zero friction.

        

      

      

        

          

          events.config
        

        

          
Events & Gigs

          
Upcoming shows with dates, venues, and ticket links. Past events auto-archive for your history.

        

      

      

        

          

          rider.config
        

        

          
Technical Rider

          
Equipment requirements, stage plot, and hospitality needs. Downloadable PDF for promoters.

        

      

      

        

          

          press.config
        

        

          
Press Quotes

          
Showcase reviews, testimonials, and media mentions. Social proof that books gigs.

        

      

      

        

          

          contact.config
        

        

          
Contact Form

          
Built-in booking enquiry form. Submissions land in your inbox, no third-party tools needed.

        

      

      

        

          

          analytics.config
        

        

          
Analytics

          
Track page views, link clicks, and visitor sources. Know who is looking at your EPK and when.

        

      

      

        

          

          domain.config
        

        

          
Custom Domains

          
Connect your own domain for a fully branded experience. SSL included, zero config required.
